## My projects
Currently, I am a third-year PhD student at the University of Bologna. 
Here the list of my research projects:
+ **Mitonuclear Coevolution** - among metazoans, with a particular focus on the impact of coevolution in shaping the phylogenetic signal.
+ **smithRNAs** - it is a class of sncRNAs encoded in the mitochondrial genome. SmithRNAs influence nuclear transcription through RNAi in the Manila clam *Ruditapes philippinarum*, our species model for this project (PI: M. Passamonti; F. Plazzi; [MitoMicro](https://sites.google.com/unisi.it/mitomicro/home)).
+ **First large-scale phylogenomics on the Gastrotricha phylum** - collaboration with the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia (Italy, PI: A. Todaro).
+ **Spiralia Phylogenomics and placement of Gastrotricha across the Tree of Life**
+ **MicroRNA annotation** in:
  + **Tardigrada** - collaboration with The Arctic University of Norway (PI: B. Fromm) and University of Modena and Reggio Emilia (PI: M. Cesari).
  + **Gastrotricha** - collaboration with The Arctic University of Norway (PI: B. Fromm) and University of Modena and Reggio Emilia (PI: A. Todaro)

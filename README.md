## My projects

I am currently a third-year PhD student at the University of Bologna.  
Here is a list of my research projects:

- **Mitonuclear coevolution** — across Metazoa, with a particular focus on how coevolution shapes phylogenetic signal.
- **smithRNAs** — a class of small non-coding RNAs encoded in the mitochondrial genome. SmithRNAs influence nuclear transcription through RNA interference in the Manila clam *Ruditapes philippinarum*, our model species for this project (PIs: M. Passamonti, F. Plazzi; [MitoMicro](https://sites.google.com/unisi.it/mitomicro/home)).
- **First large-scale phylogenomics of the Gastrotricha phylum** — in collaboration with the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia (Italy, PI: A. Todaro).
- **Spiralian phylogenomics** - with a focus on Rouphozoa.
- **microRNA annotation** in:
  - **Tardigrada** — collaboration with The Arctic University of Norway (PI: B. Fromm) and the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia (PI: M. Cesari).
  - **Gastrotricha** — collaboration with The Arctic University of Norway (PI: B. Fromm) and the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia (PI: A. Todaro).
